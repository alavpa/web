Treenovum SL

Role Full Stack Developer

This was my first company in the real world, here I've learned how businesses work. I've started as a .net developer, I came from C++/MFC so I had to learn C# and .net in record time. I should do it well because I was in this company by 9 years. At the begining I was developing all kind of webs in .net but lately I was specialized more in Sharepoint. It was a new market and in Switzerland there was a lot of demand on that technology. I was very involved in Microsoft at that moment but...

Suddenly new iPhone came out to the market and Android started to take relevance in the industry. Our company in Switzerland didn't want to miss that train so they asked me to change my expertise. I decided to move to Android, at that time Java sounded more familiar to me than objective-C and the transition could be easier.

I've developed a lot of applications for Treenovum, but one of the most importants was an internal application for PostAuto, an application used to report incidences inside the buses and trains offline/online

One of the inconvenients of this company was that at certain point I needed to grow up as developer, I felt it was the moment to move to a bigger company with a bigger team with another ways to make software. I have very good memories of that period.
