Instituto de Ingeniería Energética - UPV

Role Software Developer

At this moment I was studying computer science when a collegue asked me to join to the IIE in the Universidad Politécnica de Valencia, for sure I've needed some money at this time so after an interview I've started to work in the IMS-ART application, a new software developed by students and managed by teachers to help professionals to simulate any vapour-compression refrigeration system operating with almost any refrigerant and secondary fluid.

That product was developed in C++/MFC with Microsoft tools and was the beginning of my career as developer. Here I understood how the software works, the relevance of clean code and the effect of a good or bad performance on the user experience.

I was in this company for at least four years, I've developed my PFC in this company based on a plugin for the software able to render any kind of functions as in 2D as in 3D and also drew pipes connections to simulate the best path for fluids.

This experience was invaluable to the next step in my career, the real world and the softaware industry. When I was done with this company after 4 years, I felt ready for the next challenge.
