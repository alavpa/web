Role Android Developer

After my experience with AXA in Barcelona I felt ready to come back to Valencia with all the knowledge adquired during the past year. I've started working with the new company adquisition gigigo with the Forme Coca-Cola App for Latin and South America. I've found a very old app in Java, following some patterns like MVP but far away from a good architecture. Without dependency injection neither unit test. So, I've raised my hand and made a proposal to improve the application.

We made a new kotlin app following the SOLID principles, we started a clean architecture based in RxJava for the interactors, with Koin as dependency injector (I know, it's a service locator, but I am still don't know the difference) and MVP for the presentation layer. All this was implemented following TDD and we achieved finally a 99% crash-free. So I am very proud of the result and the company was very happy with our work.
